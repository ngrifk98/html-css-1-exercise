About the Movie

"Dune: Part Two" is the highly anticipated sequel to the 2021 film adaptation of Frank Herbert's science fiction novel, "Dune". Directed by Denis Villeneuve, the movie continues the epic journey of Paul Atreides as he navigates the treacherous and mystical world of Arrakis.
